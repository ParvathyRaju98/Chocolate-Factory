Chocolate Unwrapped

An analytic guide to your healthiest chocolate bar

Introduction to project

This project is an attempt to conduct a comparitive study to understand which Chocolate Bar is the healthiest option out there in the Market. In todays world of health and diet consciousness we tend to always read labels but alas fall for the bright packaging and false advertising. This visualisaton entails to break down the parameters of what really defines a healthy chocolate bar and tends to study market patterns to help the consumer in the same

So how do you find the healthiest chocolate bar for you?
